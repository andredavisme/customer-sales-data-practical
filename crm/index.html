The 8-Step Journey

Customer Data Flow

Every customer interaction follows a logical sequence. Every step creates a record.

01
Customer

Who is asking? The foundation of every record.

02
Request

What are they asking for? Captures the initial intent.

03
Estimate

What will it take to fulfill? Internal cost and timeline analysis.

04
Quote

What is the formal offer? The external-facing commitment.

05
Order

What has been agreed upon? The binding record of the transaction.

06
Delivery

What was delivered, and when? The moment of truth.

07
Adjustment

What changed along the way? Tracks deviations from the original agreement.

08
Response

How did the customer react? The feedback that closes the loop.
